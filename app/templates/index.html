Instructions

  • When the game starts, you will be either the Lead Expert or the Defuser.
  • In the next screen, you will learn which of the two roles you will have in the game.
  • If you become the Lead Expert, you must guide the Defuser through their map to defuse the bomb.
  • If you become the Defuser, tell you teammate where you are (the yellow square) and where the bomb is (the red triangle), then head to the bomb using the arrow keys.

Note also that:

  • You only have a limited amount of time to complete the task.
  • Upon completing the task, the Defuser must share a unique code with the Lead Expert, so that you can both be rewarded.
  • There is a questionnaire at the end of the task that must be completed.
